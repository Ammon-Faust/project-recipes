Egg In A Frame

Description:

If you love fried eggs on toast, you'll love this quick and easy version where the egg is cooked in the center of the toast!



Ingredients:

  • 1 slice bread
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • salt to taste


Instructions:

  1. Brush melted butter on both sides of bread.
  2. Cut a circle in the center of bread with a cookie cutter.
  3. Heat a small skillet over medium heat. Add bread and cook until golden brown, about 2 minutes.
  4. Flip, then crack egg into the hole. Cook until egg is set on the bottom, about 1 minute; season with salt.
  5. Cover, reduce the heat to low, and cook until egg white is completely set, 3 to 4 minutes.
