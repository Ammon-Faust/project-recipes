Peach Cobbler

Description:

An easy peach cobbler made with just 5 common ingredients you probably always have on hand?

That's right! This simple dessert comes together in minutes. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream and whipped cream.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 16 oz sliced peaches
