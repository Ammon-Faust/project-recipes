Peach Cobbler

Description:

An easy peach cobbler made with just 5 common ingredients you probably always have on hand?

That's right! This simple dessert comes together in minutes. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream and whipped cream.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 16 oz sliced peaches


Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and milk in a bowl until there are no lumps.
  2. Melt the butter in a cast-iron skillet, then top with the flour mixture.
  3. Add the peaches (with the syrup) to the skillet and stir until the peaches are evenly spread around the skillet and coated with the flour mixture.
  4. Bake peach cobbler for 30 to 45 minutes at 350 degrees F. Cooling slightly after baking ensures the cobbler sets nicely for easy serving.
  5. Optional: Add scope of vanilla icecream
